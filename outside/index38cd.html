How can I invest with Kiremo?

To make a investment you must first become a member of Kiremo. Once you are signed up, you can make your first deposit. All deposits must be made through the Members Area. You can login using the member username and password you receive when signup.

I wish to invest with Kiremo but I don't have an any ecurrency account. What should I do?

You can open a free PM account here: www.perfectmoney.is

How do I open my Kiremo Account?

It's quite easy and convenient. Follow this link, fill in the registration form and then press "Register".

Which e-currencies do you accept?

We accept Bitcoin (BTC), Etheurem (ETH) and Binance coin (BNB).

How can I withdraw funds?

Login to your account using your username and password and check the Withdraw section.

How long does it take for my deposit to be added to my account?

Your account will be updated as fast, as you deposit.

How can I change my e-mail address or password?

Send us a message on the contact form, Title: Account Update. we will process this change for you as soon as possible.

What if I can't log into my account because I forgot my password?
Due to special security reasons the Kiremo community does not offer password recovery or change.
Does a daily profit paid directly to my currency account?

No, profits are gathered on your Kiremo account and you can withdraw them anytime. At the end of your investment date, export your earnings to your Kiremo wallet then you can withdraw your cash any time.

How do you calculate the interest on my account?

Depending on each plan. Interest on your Kiremo account is acquired Daily, Weekly, Bi-Weekly, Monthly and Yearly and credited to your available balance at the end of each day.

Can I do a direct deposit from my account balance?

Yes! To make a deposit from your Kiremo account balance. Simply login into your members account and click on Make Deposit and select the Deposit from Account Balance Radio button.

Can I make an additional deposit to Kiremo account once it has been opened?

Yes, you can but you won't be allowed to deposit any cash while you have a pending deposit, Active investment plan or Incomplete Profile setup.

After I make a withdrawal request, when will the funds be available on my ecurrency account?

Funds are usually available immediately.

How can I change my password?

You can change your password by sending us a message from the support page.

Can I lose money?

There is a risk involved with investing in all high yield investment programs. However, there are a few simple ways that can help you to reduce the risk of losing more than you can afford to. First, align your investments with your financial goals, in other words, keep the money you may need for the short-term out of more aggressive investments, reserving those investment funds for the money you intend to raise over the long-term. It's very important for you to know that we are real traders and that we invest members' funds on major investments.

How can I check my account balances?

You can access the account information 24 hours, seven days a week over the Internet.

May I open several accounts in your program?

No. If we find that one member has more than one account, the entire funds will be frozen.

How can I make a spend?

To make a spend you must first become a member of Kiremo. Once you are signed up, you can make your first spend. All spends must be made through the Member Area. You can login using the member username and password you received when signup.

Who manages the funds?
